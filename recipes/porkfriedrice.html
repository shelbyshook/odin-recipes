Pork Fried Rice Recipe

An easy to prepare and fast to cook meal

Pork fried rice is a classic Asian dish that anyone can make. All you need to do is prepare your ingredients and cook them in the correct order, perferably in a cast iron wok, or any properly sized pan.

Ingredients

  • White or brown rice
  • Vegetables of your choosing (ex: carrots, peas, broccoli, onions)
  • 2 Eggs
  • Pork (or chicken for chicken fried rice)
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice in a seperate pan store in refridgerator.
  2. Perpare all vegetables by dicing them and set them aside in a plate.
  3. Cook eggs, scrambled, and set aside on a plate.
  4. Cook pork and onions on both sides until browning on the edges.
  5. Add diced vegetables and cook until ready to serve. Stir ingredients in pan regularly to avoid sticking.
