Grilled Chicken Breast Recipe

A healthy and easy to make dish

Grilled cicken breast is a classic and easy to make healthy dish.

Ingredients

  • Appropriately sized chicken breast
  • Any vegetables or sides you would like. A salad is a healthy option.
  • Salt, pepper, and any other seasonings desired.

Steps

  1. If you are making a salad, prepare the salad first by cutting up lettuce and dicing and vegetables you desire.
  2. Prepare chicken breast by cutting it to the appropriate size for whatever size serving you wish.
  3. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and any other seasonings on each side.
  4. Warm grill to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  5. Grill chicken breast for 4 minutes on each side.
  6. Make sure internal temperature of chicken is at least 165 degrees before serving.
  7. Serve chicken breast and salad on a plate. Enjoy!
